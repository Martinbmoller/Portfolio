Synk.group

Website

(2023)

Synk is a website that registers and sorts the most popular music listened to by a self-selected group of people. The songs are categorized into the appropriate genres and presented on the website using a bubble cluster chart, where the size of the circle illustrates the popularity of the genre within the group. Each genre also functions as a working playlist on Spotify, updated daily. This way, you can get to know each other better through music, and both share and discover new music together.
