Jegerrapper.no

Website

(2024)

Jegerrapper.no is a website dedicated to the Norwegian rapper Unge Obi. The website primarily serves as a collection of all his released songs, making it easier for users to learn the lyrics or analyze them more deeply if desired. The visual expression reflects the artist. The identity plays on humour, while at the same time expressing professionalism. I have both designed and coded this website myself.
